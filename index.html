About Us

1221 Capital's aim is to be exceptional stewards of capital and be the bridge that increases the financial independence of investors everywhere.

1221 Capital LLC is a Boston based investment firm registered with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission and member of the National Futures Association. As Commodity Trading Advisors, we offer individual investors as well as institutional investors the opportunity to diversify their portfolio in an asset class called Managed Futures. 1221 Capital investment programs are offered through Separately Managed Accounts. We happily work individually with investors or alongside their current team of investment advisors. The goal is to identify an investment program which will best support the clients current portfolio and investment goals as a diversification tool. When working with 1221 Capital, clients can expect prompt professional service from a dedicated account manager who will take all the time necessary for the client to have a thorough understanding of their account and investment process.

At 1221 Capital LLC, we are capitalists and view wealth creation a noble pursuit. Our investment programs offer the incredible opportunities provided in the futures markets as well as a liquid diversification solution.

1221 Capital LLC is a boutique investment firm specializing in trading commodity and financial futures. 1221 Capital offers investment programs designed to deliver consistent, positive results regardless of stock market direction, and put the odds of success firmly on the side of the investor.

Growth

Managed Futures programs have the ability to grow throughout a variety of economic conditions and historically have shown a low correlation to traditional asset classes.

Focus

At 1221 Capital, our sole purpose is managed futures. This focus has made us experts in this area, and able to introduce exceptional opportunities to our clients.

Strategy

1221 Capital’s strategies utilize mathematical techniques with an emphasis on risk versus reward to increase the probability of success for the investor.

Investment Approach

1221 Capital Managed Account programs have the ability to profit in both rising as well as declining markets.

Trend Following Program

  • Seeks to be buyers when a trend is up and sellers when a trend is down
  • Use math to increase the odds of success
  • Never guess or hope about market direction, but rather let the markets themselves tell us where they want to go
  • Dynamic Portfolio Management: Regularly analyze price trends and make appropriate portfolio changes to best position client for success
  • Sees no need to be fully invested at all times, seeks to only put money to work when most advantageous

Equity Index Trading Program

  • Invests in US Equity Index futures
  • Seeks market timing using technical analysis
  • Investment program will be long, short or cash depending on market outlook

Option Selling Program

  • Seeks to create a cash flowing asset not dependent upon price appreciation for success
  • Collect option premium for the risk taken
  • Capitalize on the time decay aspect inherent in option pricing
